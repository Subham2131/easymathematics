Measurement Formulas

Common Measurement Formulas
Measurement Type Formula Description
Area of a Rectangle A = l × w Where A is the area, l is the length, and w is the width.
Area of a Circle A = πr² Where A is the area and r is the radius.
Area of a Triangle A = ½ × b × h Where A is the area, b is the base, and h is the height.
Volume of a Cube V = a³ Where V is the volume and a is the side length.
Volume of a Rectangular Prism V = l × w × h Where V is the volume, l is the length, w is the width, and h is the height.
Volume of a Sphere V = (4/3)πr³ Where V is the volume and r is the radius.
Perimeter of a Rectangle P = 2(l + w) Where P is the perimeter, l is the length, and w is the width.
Perimeter of a Square P = 4a Where P is the perimeter and a is the side length.
Circumference of a Circle C = 2πr Where C is the circumference and r is the radius.
Speed S = d / t Where S is the speed, d is the distance, and t is the time.
Distance d = S × t Where d is the distance, S is the speed, and t is the time.
Time t = d / S Where t is the time, d is the distance, and S is the speed.
